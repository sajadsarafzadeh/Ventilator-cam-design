# Bio-Inspired Ventilator Cam Design

## Overview
This project involves the mechanical design and dynamic validation of a cam-driven ventilator mechanism. The design parameters are derived from human biometric data to ensure physiological compatibility.

## Technical Specifications
* **Biometric Basis**: Piston stroke calculated from a target **Tidal Volume** of 0.532 L (based on a 76 kg body mass).
* **Motion Profile**: Implemented **Cycloid Motion** for rise and fall phases to minimize vibration and high-frequency "jerk".
* **Mechanism**: A disc cam with a flat-faced follower integrated into a piston-cylinder assembly.

## Simulation & Analysis
* **MATLAB**: Used for analytical modeling of displacement, velocity, and acceleration.
* **SolidWorks & ADAMS**: Performed 3D motion studies and dynamic simulations to validate the MATLAB results.
* **Validation**: Comparative analysis showed high correlation between analytical equations and numerical solver outputs.

## Tools Used
